Oscar Ludeña
============

-   *Oscar Ludeña (born 28 March 1946) is a Peruvian boxer.*

Oscar Ludeña (born 28 March 1946) is a Peruvian boxer. He competed in
the men's heavyweight event at the 1972 Summer Olympics.
