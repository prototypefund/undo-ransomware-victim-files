This is a list of United States Attorneys appointed by the 45th president of the United States, Donald Trump.
As of June 19, 2019, President Trump has nominated 83 people to be U.S. Attorneys, and 81 of them have been confirmed. There are a total of 93 U.S. Attorneys in the Department of Justice.
