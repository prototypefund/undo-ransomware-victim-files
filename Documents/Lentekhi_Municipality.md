=====================

Lentekhi (Georgian: ლენტეხის მუნიციპალიტეტი, Lenṫexis municiṗaliṫeṫi) is
a district of Georgia, in the region of Racha-Lechkhumi and Kvemo
Svaneti. Its main town is Lentekhi.
