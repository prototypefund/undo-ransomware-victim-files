Octavio Juan Díaz (7 October 1900 – 12 November 1977) was an Argentine football (soccer) goalkeeper who played for the Argentina national football team.
Díaz played club football for Club Atlético Rosario Central, where he won a few local titles  and a national cup in 1920. He represented Argentina on 9 occasions between 1926 and 1928. He was a member of the Argentine team, which won the 1927 South American Championship  and the silver medal in the 1928 Olympic football tournament.
