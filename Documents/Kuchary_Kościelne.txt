    • Kuchary Kościelne [kuˈxarɨ kɔɕˈt͡ɕɛlnɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Rychwał, within Konin County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland.
Kuchary Kościelne [kuˈxarɨ kɔɕˈt͡ɕɛlnɛ] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Rychwał, within Konin County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. It lies approximately 8 kilometres (5 mi) north of Rychwał, 13 km (8 mi) south-west of Konin, and 90 km (56 mi) east of the regional capital Poznań.
