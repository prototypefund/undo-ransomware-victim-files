Fuleswari is a Bengali romance drama film directed by Tarun Majumdar based on a story of Bibhutibhushan Bandyopadhyay. This film was released on 3 May 1974 under the banner of Radharani Pictures. Hemanta Mukhopadhyay was the music director of the film.
