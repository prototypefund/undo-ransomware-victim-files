Durrës 1 (Albanian: Rajoni Nr. 1 në Durrës) is one of the six Administrative divisions in Durrës. Administrated by Roland Çobani, it has an area of 2.98 square kilometres (1.15 sq mi) and a population of 28,789. It includes four neighborhoods.
