This is a list of presidential trips made by Donald Trump during 2017, the first year of his presidency as the 45th President of the United States.
This list excludes trips made within Washington, D.C., the U.S. federal capital in which the White House, the official residence and principal workplace of the President, is located. Also excluded are trips to Camp David, the country residence of the President. International trips are included. 
The number of visits per state or territory where he traveled are:
One visit to: Alabama, Arizona, Connecticut, Delaware, Georgia, Indiana, Iowa, Kentucky, Louisiana, Michigan, Mississippi, North Carolina, North Dakota, Puerto Rico, Tennessee and Utah.
Two visits to: Hawaii, Missouri, Nevada, Ohio, South Carolina, West Virginia and Wisconsin.
Three visits to: Pennsylvania and Texas
Five visits to: Maryland
Six visits to: New York
Ten visits to: New Jersey
Twelve visits to: Florida
Thirty-one visits to: Virginia
