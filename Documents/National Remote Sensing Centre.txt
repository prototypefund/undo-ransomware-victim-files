    • National Remote Sensing Centre (Hindi: राष्ट्रीय सुदूर संवेदन केन्द्र), or NRSC, located at Shadnagar is one of the centres of the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO).
National Remote Sensing Centre (Hindi: राष्ट्रीय सुदूर संवेदन केन्द्र), or NRSC, located at Shadnagar is one of the centres of the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO). NRSC manages data from aerial and satellite sources.
