Rzuchowa [ʐuˈxɔva] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Pleśna, within Tarnów County, Lesser Poland Voivodeship, in southern Poland. It lies approximately 10 kilometres (6 mi) south of Tarnów and 74 km (46 mi) east of the regional capital Kraków.
The village has an approximate population of 2000.
