Bonkorowé
=========

-   *Bonkorowé is a village in the Sami Department of Banwa Province in
    western Burkina Faso.*

Bonkorowé is a village in the Sami Department of Banwa Province in
western Burkina Faso. As of 2005 it had a population of 330.
